Good to know

Gift card FAQ

What can a TutCasa gift card be used for?

It can be applied toward any TutCasa stay, and toward concierge services, tours and experiences — the recipient chooses how to use it.

How is it delivered?

Digital cards are delivered by email — instantly, or on a date you schedule. You can also print a card at home to give in person.

Does it expire?

Gift cards are valid for 12 months from the date of purchase. We’ll always try to work with you if you’re close to the date.

Are there any fees?

No fees. The full value goes toward the recipient’s booking — the price you pay is the value they get.

Can it be used partially?

Yes. If a stay costs more than the card, the balance is simply paid at booking. If it costs less, the remaining value stays on the card.

How does the recipient redeem it?

They enter the gift code at checkout, or send it to May on WhatsApp and we’ll apply it to their booking.
